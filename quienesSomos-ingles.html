History

Perfect for packaging that aims to convey a rich story and strong traditional value, this circular logo functions as a denomination-of-origin seal that guarantees authenticity. The richness of the illustrative details—mountains, plantations, and coffee cherries—visually immerses the consumer in the Coffee Axis landscape at the point of sale. Whether it highlights the raw bean (ideal for whole-bean coffee or home roasting) or the steaming cup (perfect for ground or instant coffee), this design creates an image of tradition, authenticity, and harvest freshness—ideal for brands that celebrate their heritage and process.
